The Best Movies According to Rafa

My top 3 movies of all time.

To All The Boys

I love this movie series since its a Rom-com and has a very cute love story.

20th Century Girl

Its a tragic romance. I love this movie's aesthetic and a wonderfull storyline.

Bangalore Days

This movie is a whole vibe and something different I have seen in Malayalam Cinema. Its a clean, fun, romantic and sentimental movie.
